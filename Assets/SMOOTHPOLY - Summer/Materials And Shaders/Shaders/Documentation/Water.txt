Water shader.

Shader properties:

Metallic: Change the metalnes value.
Smoothness: Change the smoothness value.
BaseColor: Choose the base color
RipplesColor: Choose the color of the ripples.
RippleSpeed: Change the speed of ripples moving.
RippleDissolve: Change the speed of ripples dissolving.
NormalStrength: Change the amount of normal force here.
NoiseStrength: Noise texture is chanelled to give normal value to material. Change the strength here.
NoiseOffset: You can change the movement of the noise texture.
NoiseTiling: Scale or change the tiling of the noise.
